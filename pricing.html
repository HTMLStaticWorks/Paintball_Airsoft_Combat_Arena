Frequently Asked Questions

Operational Intel & Guidelines

What is included in the basic rental package?
The basic rental includes a standard mechanical marker, a thermal safety mask, a tactical chest protector, and your first hopper of 500 paintballs (or 2000 BBs for Airsoft).
Do you offer group discounts for parties?
Yes! For groups of 10 or more, we offer the Squad Rate which provides a significant discount per operator. Larger events qualify for our Elite Platoon and Full Battalion packages.
Can I bring my own paintballs or BBs?
To ensure the safety of our players and maintain the quality of our arenas, we are a field-paint only facility. You must purchase tournament-grade paintballs and BBs on-site.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
We require a 48-hour notice for any cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours will be credited towards a future deployment date.
Are there any age restrictions for players?
Players must be at least 10 years old to participate in standard operations. We offer low-impact scenarios specifically designed for younger players aged 8-12.
