> MISSION FILE: 884-A

VIP Escort Protocol

Protect the high-value target and extract them safely through hostile territory.

Mission Briefing

In this high-stakes scenario, one team is designated as the protection detail. Among their ranks is an unarmed VIP (indicated by specialized high-visibility gear). The protection detail must navigate from the insertion point to the designated extraction zone, ensuring the VIP survives.

Primary Objectives

  • ■ Escort Team: Move the VIP to the extraction point within the time limit. The VIP must be "alive" upon reaching the zone.
  • ■ Assault Team: Eliminate the VIP before they reach extraction.

Tactical Considerations

Communication is critical. The VIP cannot return fire, making them a liability that requires constant 360-degree security. The assault team can employ ambushes and choke points, forcing the escort team to rely on smoke, suppressive fire, and flanking maneuvers.

Deployment Intel

Recommended Team Size 6 vs 6 (Min)
Time Limit 15 Minutes
